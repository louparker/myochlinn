Support

Returns & Exchange Policy

We hopes you will be absolutely delighted with your jewellery. If for any reason you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase we are happy to offer an exchange or credit note within 30 days of purchase.
Alternatively, we can refund a sale made through this website if it is returned unworn, in perfect condition, and in its original packaging within 14 working days of your receipt of the order.

Purchases exempt from both the refund and exchange policies, unless they are structurally faulty, are:

  • Made to order pieces
  • Commissioned pieces (bespoke)
  • Customised pieces
​To return an online purchase, please contact hello@ellismhairicameron.com for further details. Please include your order number in the email.
In the interests of fraud prevention, your refund will be issued by the same method by which you paid within 28 days of receipt of the return.

FAQ

For Returns, please see the 'Shipping & Returns' section.

Delivery time varies depending on the collection. You will receive the product within 1-2 working days within Sweden, and 2-5 working days for other countries.
Maximum delivery time is 8 weeks if the item has been made to order, you will be emailed if that is the case.
If you have left it a bit late and need something sooner than the delivery time stated, or by a specific date, then just email info@mylinn.com.

Each piece of jewellery is completely handmade in our Stockholm workshop, so every piece is unique. Due to this, small variations will occur with each piece, resulting in slight differences from photographs you may have seen. Colours may vary marginally, and there may be small differences in stone size.

If you’re looking for something completely unique, within the My & Linn aesthetic, we offers a private consultation with us in person, via video chat, or over the phone. This is an exclusive opportunity to learn about our creative process and bring to life an original piece of jewellery for you to treasure.

Every piece made by us is designed to be worn and loved everyday, but as with all fine jewellery, it should be treated with care. We provides a one year warranty on all pieces of jewellery from the date of purchase, provided it has not been worked on by another jeweller or if the damage goes beyond accepted patterns of wear. Beyond the warranty period, we will quote for any necessary repairs on a case by case basis.

If your item was delivered or received damaged please notify us within 48 hours so that we can look into it. It would help us out if you could take 1 or 2 photos of the issue and email it to us along with the order number and a quick description. Don't worry though, we will be able to send you a replacement as quickly as possibly once we have determined the issue. Please note we will likely need you to send us back the damaged goods.

Our is made from 100% recycled metal. The Silver pieces are made of solid 925 Sterling Silver and the Gold pieces are Gold Vermeil, consisting of Sterling Silver with 3 microns of 18k Gold plating.
