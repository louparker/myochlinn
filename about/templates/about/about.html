ABOUT US

Sister Act

Having learned at a young age from their parents the art of creating something beautiful out of a lump of clay, the two sisters expanded their horizons and got into jewelry making, silversmithing and goldsmithing. They have created pieces for some of Swedens biggest musicians and actors & they continue to generate a hype on social media with their trendy unique pieces.

My

My is a quiet, unassuming force of nature . With an ability to create prestine pieces of jewelry out of seemingly unusable bits of material, she really is taking her craft to the next level.

Linn

Linn is a wondering soul brought back to Stockholm by the ongoing pandemic. She takes in cultures from all over the world and attempts to output them in whatever she creates.
